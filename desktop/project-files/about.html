Photographer

Hi, my name is Lindsey Grace! I'm a professional photographer and owner/operator of Heavenly Angels Portrait Studio in Louisville, KY. Capturing the precious milestones and memories of your loved ones that you will cherish for a lifetime, is my absolute passion! I am available weekends for weddings, graduations, celebrations, outdoor photo shoots, and glamour shoots. I do freelance work on the side and am available for hire. I look forward to hearing from you!

Portrait Studio

Heavenly Angels Portrait Studio was founded by photographer Lindsey Grace in 2007 and is located at 2125 Snap Blvd in the East End neighborhood of Middletown in Louisville, KY. Heavenly Angels is a family-based portrait studio, mostly taking pictures of infants, toddlers, school-aged children, and families. However, we also photograph for church groups, businesses, the police, the military, etc. The sky is the limit at Heavenly Angels and we look forward to meeting all of your photography needs. Appointments are not necessary, but schedule ahead to avoid the wait!
